About Us

    SenseSeeWare is a Toronto-based start-up developing an assistive smart wearable device for the visually impaired. We oﬀer state-of-the-art proven AI-based technologies such as Assistive Vibrotactile Guidance, Object Recognition, and Smart Reader. Our innovative In-house built software helps partially or fully blind individuals perform daily tasks such as reading, walking, and finding desired objects.

    Our Product

    Our product presents a number of innovative software and technologies that detect, classify, and recognize over 100,000 objects and help a visually impaired individual recognize friends, find and walk toward an object, and read text such as books or cooking instructions. Our device is comprised of two high-performance cameras, various advanced sensors for multiple use cases such as depth and distance measurement, and a high-speed micro-computer. All are enclosed in a compact case that is wearable on the user's wrist. A major feature of our device is the Assistive Vibrotactile Guidance (AVG) technology which guides the user’s hand towards an object using a number of micro-vibrators built into the wearable device. The AVG technology has the ability to guide the user within the 3-dimensional space in a very intuitive manner. Our device accepts voice commands or hand gestures to interact with the user.

    We aim to make our device helpful, practical, accessible, and affordable to the blind community across Canada and around the world.
